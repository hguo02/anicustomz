About Me!

Hello friends!

My name is Anika Ho. I’m a first year Arts Business student studying law and business at the University of Waterloo. I’ve been drawing and painting for the majority of my life and have always gravitated to painting. I stumbled across the art of sneaker customizations in late 2019 and thought I might try it myself. When quarantine hit in March break of 2020, I had a lot of free time on my hands and decided to buy some leather paint and do a pair of customs for my wonderful friend Victoria. I fell in love with the feeling of gratification and pride in my finished works, and have continued since!

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Do you only customize Air Force 1 lows?

A: As of October 25th, I’m only customizing triple white AF1’s. I’m looking to begin customizing black AF1’s for customers soon! I will post updates on the website as well as my Instagram!

Q: Where are you located?

A: From Sept 2020 - May 2021, I’m based in both Scarborough and Waterloo for school.

Q: Does your pricing include shoes? Do you buy the shoes, or should I buy and drop them off?

A: I am open to either option!

  • If you choose to have me buy the kicks, I will purchase the shoes at retail price and the cost will be added to your total.
  • If you choose to buy the shoes, you may either ship them to my address which I will give you upon contact and discussion, or (if you bought them already in person or had them shipped to you) you can drop them off at the provided address yourself and I will retrieve them.


Q: Can I give you used shoes to customize? I’ve only worn my shoes twice, is that alright?

A: Used shoes are cool with me! I have no issue with customizing used shoes, and have cleaned and customized more than a couple pairs. But do be mindful to clean out things you’ve stepped in like goose dookie. If you’ve severely creased a part of the shoe you want customized, that may complicate things a tad but I’m very flexible! Contact me if you're unsure!

Q: How long does it take to complete the order?

A: I’ve been navigating how long it takes me to complete orders, and in the past, it really depended on my workload from school, and unprecedented personal situations. But going forward, now that I’ve learnt to better manage my time, orders should take 1-5 weeks.

Q: Do you do realism?

A: I am not currently doing any realism. Realism takes a lot of time, and frankly I don’t have the time to spend all the effort and hours I’d like to make something I’m proud of.

Q: What do you use to paint?

A: I use Angelus leather acrylic paints!

Q: Are the designs water-proof?

A: Yes!!! I use the Angelus finisher with added duller to finish and seal my designs which makes them scratchproof. Then I use crep protect to assure they are waterproofed as well.
